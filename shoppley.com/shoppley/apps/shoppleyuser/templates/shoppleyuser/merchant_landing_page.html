Sending Offers
  1. Add 508-690-0888 to your contacts and save as “Shoppley”.
  2. Txt #offer with the details of the offer in 150 characters (offer will be valid for 90 mins).
  3. You will receive a tracking number (via txt and e-mail).
Good Offer Examples:
$5 off 28-34 sized pants in stock
20% off freshly baked bread this afternoon
FREE orange juice with a sandwich purchase
 
 
Redeem
  1. Customers will soon visit with their Shoppley offer code.
  2. Txt #redeem with the customer’s offer code and their cell phone number.
  3. This helps you track the performance of your offer.
 
 
Track
  1. Txt #status with your tracking number to find out the number of customers that were reached and redeemed.
  2. Improve your offers based on our feedback.
  3. Soon you will be the master in sending out offers that make your customers happy!
