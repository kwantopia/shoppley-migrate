Sending Offers
  1. Save 508-690-0888 in your cellphone as shoppley.
  2. Txt ‘offer’ and the detail of the offer in 160 characters to start the 90 min timer.
  3. You will receive a tracking number txt and email.
Good Offer Examples:
20% off freshly baked bread this afternoon.
FREE orange juice with a sandwich purchase.
 
 
Redeeming Offers
  1. Txt ‘redeem’ with each customer's offer code and their cellphone number.
  2. If the offer was valid, you will receive a reply.
 
 
Track Customers
  1. Txt ‘status’ and your offer code to shoppley to find out number of customers that were reached.
  2. Based on the numbers, you can try providing different offers at different times.
  3. Soon you will be master in sending offers to maximize your profit.
