Shoppley {% trans "FAQ" %}

I am a store owner, how does Shoppley help me?
Shoppley helps you by pulling customers on-demand in the next 90 minutes. You decide on the offer to send out. Just text message Shoppley and Shoppley will send it to potential customers that may come visit you in the next 90 minutes. You will get immediate feedback if anybody is reached or if nobody is available.
What are some good offer examples?
  • #offer $5 off 28-34 sized pants in stock
  • #offer 20% off freshly baked bread this afternoon
  • #offer FREE orange juice with a sandwich purchase
I am a regular user (consumer), how does Shoppley help me?
You will receive an serendipitous offer from a local merchant that will provide you with a timely deal. The more you redeem it, the better the offers will become.
Does it cost to use Shoppley?
No, it is currently in Alpha and there is no cost to using Shoppley except for the regular text message charges by your mobile operator. If you have unlimited text you will not be charged.
As a consumer, how many messages will I be receiving a day?
For Alpha, you will receive one to five messages a day. If you want to receive more, you can adjust your profile settings. As new features become functional, you will be able to decide on how many offers you want to receive on a day.
My friend forwarded me an offer and a Shoppley account was automatically created. Am I automatically signed up?
You are signed up but you will not be receiving any further offers from Shoppley until you login to the account and verify your account. If your Shoppley friends forward you future offers, you will still receive those offers.
Why another deal site?
I have not used existing deal sites because what they send me is not what I actually want or need at this moment. I have bought Groupons and never used it. Shoppley uses a proprietary method that brings real time offers that you can use now and it will only get better with time.
